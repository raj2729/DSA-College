APPLICATIONS OF STACKS
____________________________________________________

In this section we will discuss typical problems where stacks can be easily applied for a simple
and efficient solution. The topics that will be discussed in this section include the following:

1. Reversing a list(Enter numbers and display stack . This reverses it )

2. Parentheses checker

3. Conversion of an infix expression into a postfix expression

4. Evaluation of a postfix expression

5. Conversion of an infix expression into a prefix expression

6. Evaluation of a prefix expression

7. Recursion

8. Tower of Hanoi
